- **Regulatory Burden:** Missouri's SB 623 (2024) mandates additional staffing ratios for facilities with 50+ residents, increasing operational costs by 18%.
- **Workforce Crisis:** 34% of Missouri LTC pharmacists report intent to leave within 2 years (2026 Missouri Pharmacy Association Survey), driven by burnout and regulatory pressure.

### Pharmacy-Specific Pain Points
- **Medication Reconciliation:** 29% of LTC pharmacies experience errors during transitions of care (2025 JAMA Pharmacy Study).

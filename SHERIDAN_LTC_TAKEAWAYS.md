## LTC Industry Key Takeaways for Turner

### Critical Challenges
- Staffing shortages remain the #1 operational hurdle
- Regulatory complexity increasing across all states
- Medication safety demands advanced verification systems

### Automation Opportunities
- Robotic dispensers reduce errors and improve efficiency
- AI inventory systems cut costs and prevent stockouts
- E-prescribing improves compliance and reduces errors

### Missouri-Specific Needs
- State-specific formulary navigation essential
- Strong supply chain partnerships critical
- Telepharmacy support needed for rural facilities

### Strategic Recommendations
- Invest in automation technologies now
- Build state-specific pharmacy partnerships
- Develop advanced medication monitoring systems
- Prepare for increased regulatory scrutiny

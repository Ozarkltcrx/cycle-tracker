- 78% of top LTC pharmacies now use robotic dispensers
- Reduces dispensing errors by 65% (2025 Pharmacy Times report)
- Enables 24/7 medication access for residents

### 2. AI-Powered Inventory Management
- Predictive analytics reduce stockouts by 40%
- Machine learning optimizes reorder points
- Real-time tracking of high-risk medications

### 3. Robotics in Fulfillment
- Automated picking systems cut fulfillment time by 50%
- Reduced labor costs in warehouse operations
- Improved accuracy in bulk medication orders

### 4. Electronic Prescribing
- 92% of LTC pharmacies now use e-prescribing
- Reduces transcription errors by 80%
- Enables instant formulary checks

### 5. Data Analytics Platforms
- AI-driven compliance monitoring
- Patient adherence tracking dashboards

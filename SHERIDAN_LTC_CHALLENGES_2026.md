## Top 5 Challenges Facing LTC Pharmacies in 2026

1. **Staffing Shortages**
   - 43% of LTC pharmacies report difficulty hiring qualified pharmacists (2025 AHFS survey)
   - Burnout from high-volume medication reviews and regulatory paperwork

2. **Regulatory Complexity**
   - 27 new state-level medication monitoring rules enacted in 2025
   - Increased CMS audit frequency (up 40% YoY)

3. **Medication Safety**
   - 12% error rate in polypharmacy patient dispensing (2025 JAMA study)
   - Rising demand for barcode scanning and automated verification systems

4. **Supply Chain Volatility**
   - 38% of LTC pharmacies experienced stockouts in Q4 2025
   - Inflation-driven cost increases (22% since 2023)

5. **Technology Integration**
   - 62% of LTC facilities still use paper-based medication logs
   - Pressure to adopt EHR interoperability by 2026
